    Chatti Choru

    It's a tasty mix of rice, chicken, beef, fish, egg, mutton, vegetable curry, and pickle—all wrapped together in a banana leaf.

    Each bite is packed with bold spices and mouthwatering flavors that come together perfectly. Whether you're craving something spicy, savory, or satisfying, Chattichor has it all in one delicious meal. Give it a try, and you’ll keep coming back for more!

    Pulao and Chicken

    Chicken Pulao is a delicious and aromatic rice dish where juicy pieces of chicken are cooked with basmati rice, whole spices, onions, and herbs, creating a perfect blend of mild flavors and rich fragrance

    It's lighter than biryani but just as satisfying, making it an ideal choice for lunch, dinner, or even special gatherings. Every spoonful offers a comforting taste of home, with tender chicken and fluffy, spice-infused rice that's sure to warm your heart and please your palate.

    Shawai mandhi

    Shawai Mandi is a flavorful Arabian rice dish made with fragrant basmati rice and tender, smoky grilled chicken (shawai), seasoned with traditional spices and served with a tangy tomato chutney. The juicy grilled chicken on top of the subtly spiced rice creates a perfect balance of taste and aroma, making Shawai Mandi a must-try for anyone who loves bold yet comforting flavors.

    Chicken and Beef Shawarma

    Chicken and beef shawarma wrapped in soft kuboos or thin romali rotti is a delicious street food favorite, filled with juicy, spiced meat, fresh veggies, creamy garlic sauce, and a burst of flavors in every bite. Whether you choose the fluffy kuboos or the light romali rotti, each wrap offers a mouthwatering experience that's perfect for a quick, satisfying meal.
